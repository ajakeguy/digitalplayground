Digital Playground - in progress

Hey Friends! This goal of this site is to consolidate educational content, tools, and information from random corners of the internet to help individuals learn about Bitcoin and the privacy-focused tools that are out there. As we transition away from centralized technology services to a Bitcoin Standard and FOSS-powered Information Age, it's important to know the tools that are out there for individuals to regain control over their digital lives. Control over your digital life = freedom in the digital age. Hopefully this site can serve as a launching pad to help shorten the learning curve for those willing to put in the work. Decentralized digital playgrounds sound better to me than centralized digital cages.

Housekeeping

Everyone has the choice to learn. There are endless amounts of content, guides, and tools that have been created to help those willing to put in the work. This ~digital playground~ consolidates information that's been helpful to me along this (never-ending) journey. The goal is to continue to shorten the learning curve for those just starting. That said, don’t trust this site and DYOR to understand the trade-offs. This research is just a tool for education, not financial or technical advice.
